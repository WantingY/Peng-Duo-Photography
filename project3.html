Sunset


Sunset


In the summer of 2013, I bring my wife from Beijing to San Francisco with me to finish my Master degree after a gap year in China. It was her first time in US, so I was trying to find the most convenience neighborhood for us to stay. Sunset, with the majority of Chinese Immigrants in the city, became a good choice.


I can still remember the day we found our apartment. I was looking at the scene out of the window of our living room, that is when the fog coming. “Look,” I talked to her, “it feels like home.”
